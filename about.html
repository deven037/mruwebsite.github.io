About US

We are the Faridabad's largest University

Manav Rachna University (MRU) is a State Private University (established by Haryana State Legislature Act No 26 of 2014 & under section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956) and has evolved from Manav Rachna College of Engineering (MRCE, established in 2004), a NAAC Accredited 'A' Grade Institution.
